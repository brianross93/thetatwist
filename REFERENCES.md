# Primary references

1. Eyal Markman, *Secant sheaves and Weil classes on abelian varieties*,
   arXiv:2509.23403v2, especially Section 12, pp. 21–22.
   <https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.23403>

2. Eyal Markman, *Cycles on abelian 2n-folds of Weil type from secant sheaves
   on abelian n-folds*, arXiv:2502.03415v2, especially Example 8.2.3 and
   Equation (8.2.3).
   <https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.03415>

The repository does not contain copies of the papers. The calculation uses
the constructions in the papers and exact rational arithmetic.
